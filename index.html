WHAT IS HACKIt?

HackIt is an innovation-driven hackathon organized by the Aikyam Club at Amity University Patna. Students participate by submitting creative ideas and solutions to given problem statements. The best innovations are rewarded with exciting prizes, recognition, and opportunities to showcase technical skills and teamwork.
